PyTuner is a desktop-based guitar tuner that is fully coded in Python.

Requirements: 
Python>=3.8.3
sounddevice>=0.4.1
scipy>=1.6.0
numpy>=1.19.2

This tuner allows you to play a note on the virtual guitar and you
can manually match it back. You can also use the Automatic tuner which
tells you whether or not a string is properly tuned.
